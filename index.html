About Us

About The Founder & Company

Founded by visionary entrepreneur Pramod Kumar Rai, PPT Car Rental has grown to become Delhi’s most trusted and reliable travel partner. Built on a foundation of years of dedicated experience in the transportation industry, our company was established with a clear, unwavering vision: to redefine the way people travel by providing premium, fully transparent, and completely hassle-free car rental services across the National Capital Region and beyond.

We understand that a journey is about much more than just getting from point A to point B—it is about the experience, the comfort, and your peace of mind. That is why we constantly evaluate and upgrade our extensive fleet of vehicles. From compact sedans for zipping through city traffic to spacious premium SUVs for family vacations, every car is meticulously inspected, well-maintained, and sanitized to ensure your absolute safety and luxury.

Our team of professional, background-verified drivers are the backbone of our service. They are highly skilled at navigating both bustling city streets and long highway routes, and are trained to provide courteous, punctual, and highly respectful service to every guest.

Whether you need a quick local drop-off, a dedicated car for a full day of corporate meetings, or a comfortable vehicle for a long outstation getaway, PPT Car Rental has you covered. We pride ourselves on combining this top-tier service with the most competitive and honest rates in the city—meaning no hidden fees, no last-minute surprises, just a smooth ride every time.
