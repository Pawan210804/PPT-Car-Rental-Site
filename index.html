About The Founder & Company

Founded by visionary entrepreneur Pramod Kumar Rai, PPT Car Rental has grown to become Delhi’s most trusted and reliable travel partner. Built on a foundation of years of dedicated experience in the transportation industry, our company was established with a clear, unwavering vision: to redefine the way people travel by providing premium, fully transparent, and completely hassle-free car rental services across the National Capital Region and beyond.

We understand that a journey is about much more than just getting from point A to point B—it is about the experience, the comfort, and your peace of mind. That is why we constantly evaluate and upgrade our extensive fleet of vehicles. From compact sedans for zipping through city traffic to spacious premium SUVs for family vacations, every car is meticulously inspected, well-maintained, and sanitized to ensure your absolute safety and luxury.

Our team of professional, background-verified drivers are the backbone of our service. They are highly skilled at navigating both bustling city streets and long highway routes, and are trained to provide courteous, punctual, and highly respectful service to every guest.

Whether you need a quick local drop-off, a dedicated car for a full day of corporate meetings, or a comfortable vehicle for a long outstation getaway, PPT Car Rental has you covered. We pride ourselves on combining this top-tier service with the most competitive and honest rates in the city—meaning no hidden fees, no last-minute surprises, just a smooth ride every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the driver included in the price?

Yes, absolutely. All our packages include a professional, background-verified driver. You never drive yourself — sit back and enjoy the journey.

Are toll charges and parking included?

No — toll, parking, and state entry taxes are charged at actuals and paid directly at the booths. We will always inform you in advance. There are no hidden service fees on top.

Can I book a car for outstation trips like Agra or Jaipur?

Yes! Outstation trips are one of our specialties. We cover Agra, Jaipur, Haridwar, Shimla, Chandigarh, Manali and more. Outstation packages include a limit of 250 km per day — kilometres beyond that are charged at the applicable per-km rate. WhatsApp us your travel dates for a custom quote.

What happens if I need the car for more than 8 hours or 80 km?

Extra hours and kilometres are charged at fixed rates — ₹15/km and ₹150/hr for Sedan, ₹22/km and ₹250/hr for Innova Crysta. No surprise calculations — we tell you upfront.

How do I confirm my booking?

Fill the booking form on this page or WhatsApp us directly. We confirm within 30 minutes during business hours. For urgent bookings, always call or WhatsApp for fastest response.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes. Please inform us at least 4 hours before your pickup time. Late cancellations may attract a small fee. WhatsApp us and we will always try to accommodate your needs.

Are the cars AC? Are they clean and sanitized?

All our vehicles are fully air-conditioned and are cleaned and sanitized before every trip. We take hygiene and your comfort very seriously.
